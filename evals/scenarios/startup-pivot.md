# Scenario: Startup Pivot Decision

## Question

"I'm running a B2B SaaS startup with 50 customers and $30k MRR. Our biggest customer (40% of revenue) wants us to build a feature that would pivot us toward a different market. Do we build it?"

## Type

Decision-making (strategic, multi-stakeholder)

## Expected Role Categories

- Strategic/business analysis
- Risk assessment
- Customer/market perspective
- Possibly: financial modeling, devil's advocate

## What This Tests

- Does the team avoid a generic "it depends" answer?
- Do agents engage with the specific numbers (40% concentration, $30k MRR)?
- Does someone flag the revenue concentration risk independently of the pivot question?
- Is the final synthesis a decision framework, not just a list of pros and cons?

## Role Rubric Overrides

- All roles: Specificity is critical. Vague startup advice ("focus on product-market fit") is a Fail.
- Devil's advocate (if present): must attack the strongest argument, not the weakest.
