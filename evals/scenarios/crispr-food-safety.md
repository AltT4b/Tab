# Scenario: CRISPR in Food Production

## Question

"Should CRISPR-edited foods be regulated differently than traditional GMOs? What does the science say, and what are the policy implications?"

## Type

Research + policy analysis (multi-angle)

## Expected Role Categories

- Research/evidence-gathering (needs web search)
- Regulatory/policy analysis
- Risk assessment or skeptic
- Possibly: industry/economic perspective

## What This Tests

- Can the team separate scientific evidence from policy opinion?
- Does the researcher cite real sources vs. generate plausible-sounding claims?
- Does the team avoid collapsing into uniform agreement?
- Is the synthesis actionable or just a summary of "it's complicated"?

## Role Rubric Overrides

- Research roles: Specificity and Honesty are weighted highest. Uncited claims are an automatic Fail on Honesty.
